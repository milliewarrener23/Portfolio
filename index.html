About Me

I have a 2:1 BSc degree in Bioveterinary Science, whcih is where I first experienced data analysis and loved it. I took part in a module called research methods where I was given large data sets linking to the agricultural industry and had to clean and analyse them using Genstat to provide valuble insights on the data and make reccommendations. I really enjoyed the module and knew it was something I wanted to do career wise, in my exam I got 96%. During my final year I undertook a dissertation titled Comparing Fructose, Glucose Sucrose, and Trehalose as Non-Permeating Cryoprotectants on the Integrity of Cryopreserved Equine Sperm. During the project I had to analyse a set of data collected by myself to answer my research aims and objectives. I used a using a repeated measures ANOVA linear mixed model where stallion ID was nested in ejaculate number. My overall project was graded at 70%.
